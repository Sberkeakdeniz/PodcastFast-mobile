Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start creating my first podcast?

After signing in, tap the "Generate" button on the home screen to start creating your podcast content. Our AI assistant will guide you through the process, helping you generate content and structure your episode.

What's included in the Pro subscription?

Pro subscription includes unlimited podcast episodes, AI-powered content generation, custom podcast cover art creation, advanced analytics, premium templates, and priority support.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your App Store settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > PodcastFast, and select "Cancel Subscription".

Can I change my account email?

Currently, email addresses cannot be changed. If you need to use a different email, please contact our support team.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by tapping "Change Password" in your profile settings. You'll need to verify your current password before setting a new one.
